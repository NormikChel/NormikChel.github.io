About the Game

Minecraft is a wonderful sandbox game where you can build, explore, and survive in an endless world. Create your own adventures and share them with friends! Unfortunately, in this thriving game, there are some "BUTs" that can be found on the Internet.

  • Open world for exploration
  • Diverse biomes
  • Diverse blocks and items
  • Multiplayer mode
